# Scenario Notes

## Emergency Observation Dispatch
- Mission-critical contracts under fire, flood, and evacuation pressure.
- The main contrast is local urgency chasing versus long-run policy compliance.

## Rare High-Value Crop Monitoring
- Rare or diseased crop parcels must not be starved by abundant background parcels.
- The main contrast is protected service for small but important contracts.

## Urban Compliance Monitoring
- School safety, industrial emissions, river protection, and construction oversight compete for revisit budget.
- The main contrast is policy-critical persistence versus convenient local reactions.
